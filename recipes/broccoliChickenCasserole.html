Broccoli Chicken Casserole

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Broccoli
  • Stuffing Mix(use your favorite boxed stuffing here)
  • Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Cook the chicken

    Simmer chicken in a large pot with water until it's no longer pink inside, about 15 minutes. Shred the chicken. If you're using leftover chicken or rotisserie chicken, simply shred it (no need to cook it again, it will warm up in the oven).

  2. Make the Soup Base

    Mix the cream of mushroom soup with the mayonnaise.

  3. Assemble casserole

    combine the soup mixture with the Chicken, broccoli, and Cheddar cheese. Transfer it to a 9x13-inch baking dish and it top with the stuffing mix.

  4. Bake the casserole

    Bake in 350 degree F oven for about 25-30 min, until the stuffing is golden brown.
